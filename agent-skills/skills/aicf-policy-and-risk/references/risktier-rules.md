# Risk Tier Rules

- Low: read-only, narrow, no sensitive data.
- Medium: scoped data access, workflow preparation, or moderate operational impact.
- High: money movement, personal data, external messages, destructive behavior, or
  permission-sensitive work.
- Critical: irreversible or broad high-impact action, privileged account changes, or
  regulated data exposure.

When in doubt, infer the higher risk and require explicit controls.
